Product

We will use product branding by creating compelling brand stories and developing brand loyalty. Specifically, we will craft brand stories by sharing meaningful testimonials from families that emphasise how our service honours heritage and ensures a dignified resting place. In addition, we will build brand loyalty through personalised consultations and exclusive events, enhancing customer satisfaction and encouraging positive word-of-mouth referrals.

Price

We will use Premium Pricing and Bundled Pricing strategies. Premium Pricing will be implemented to reflect the exclusivity and high quality of our cemetery plots, appealing to customers who value tradition and are willing to invest in a prestigious final resting place. Bundled Pricing will offer packages that include additional services such as customised memorials or floral arrangements, providing added value for customers seeking convenience and a complete end-of-life arrangement.
