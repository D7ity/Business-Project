Pareto Principle

CRegarding our business revolving around graveyard plots, our priority regarding customers will be guided by the Pareto Principle, where we focus on identifying the top 20% of customers, which would be those who purchase larger plots, premium servers or expensive pre-need arrangements, who evidently generate 80% of the revenue. By prioritising these high value customers, we can increase their satisfaction and loyalty resulting in them referring to their rich connections to our business, or purchase more from us. At the same time, streamlined and efficient processes will serve the remaining 80% of customers where we ensure they receive quality servers without extensive resource allocation, ultimately maximising business profitability while ensuring our customers aren't feeling neglected during their significant decision
